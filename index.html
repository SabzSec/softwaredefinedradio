Greetings !

We are a collection of software-defined-radio (SDR) enthusiasts – from novice to experts – who have come together with a common interest in this exciting field. If you would like to keep up-to-date with the South Indian SDR Users group discussion, please consider connecting with us or participating in our annual South Indian User Group Workshop on SDR. A place for you to tell a story and let your users know a little more about you. If you would like to stay up-to-date with the plans for SI-SDR-UG's activities, please connect with us!
